3shape website

3Shape
Client
Service
UI/UX
3Shape is a Danish company that makes best in-class intraoral scanners, lab scanners and software for dentists. They won 6 Red Dot awards for their products.
I worked for a many times on this project in different periods. The main website has many challenges and one of them is a lot of stakeholders that involved in decision making. Another thing is 7-9 languages for a localization content. Also, integrations with every other platforms in 3Shape family.

The specifics

The original task was to design a new website that will match a level of their products. Also the website will be a main lead-generative platform for new consumers who want to buy 3Shape products. The old website wat to bad and couldn't handle the new and challenging demands of growth.

To do: A professional and modern design that matches the level of 3Shape's products; A user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate; A focus on the products and services of the company; A mobile-friendly design; A lead-generation system to capture contact information from potential customer; localized content to 7-9 languages.

Language selector

At some point I was given a task to redesign a current language selector because many stakeholders expressed desire to improve UX with the old one. The complexity of the task was that we had 3-5 platform with 7-9 languages on each that should have the same unified Country selector with some small local differences.
I did the whole project from UX research, prototype, usability testing, and the final high-fidelity designs.
