Unfold is a 3Shape event that introduces the latest offerings – those designed to improve the dental practice at every step of the professional journey. Three journeys to explore. Each comprehensively covered.

2 days event with 3 different journeys, all on-demand: announcement videos and webinars8 countries pages with local content and promosLive 1:1 demos and Q&A in languages.

The Project Strategy

Create designs, that will resonate with users and involve them to explor. In total we had 5000 sign-ups (6500+ reached). Reach early majority and promote the new subscription model Promote latest updates and increase upgrade sales.

The Design Process

I delved into the project by first understanding the event's objectives and audience. The concept was to tell a story - one that unfolded the potential of 3Shape solutions. This led to the decision of crafting a design that seamlessly blended storytelling with informative content.
The designs were more than just aesthetics; they were conduits for meaningful content. Every element was meticulously chosen to align with the narrative. Our focus was on highlighting not just features, but the actual benefits that our solutions brought to the table.
The event's global nature required localization efforts. To accommodate a diverse audience, most sessions were made available in nine languages, making sure that language wasn't a barrier to participation.
