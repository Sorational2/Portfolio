Unfold event

3Shape
Client
Service
UI Design
Unfold is a 3Shape event that introduces the latest offerings – those designed to improve dental practice at every step of the professional journey. Three journeys to explore. Each is comprehensively covered.
• A new event campaign with latest company features and updates;
• 2 days event with 3 different journeys, all on-demand;
• Complex user flows: announcement videos and webinars, 8 countries pages with local content and promos, Live 1:1 demos and Q&A in languages.

The Project Strategy

Create designs, that will resonate with users and involve them to explore. In total, we had 5000 sign-ups (6500+ reached). Reach early majority and promote the new subscription model Promote latest updates and increase upgrade sales.

The Design Process

I delved into the project by first understanding the event's objectives and audience. The concept was to tell a story - one that unfolded the potential of 3Shape solutions. This led to the decision to craft a design that seamlessly blended storytelling with informative content.
The designs were more than just aesthetics; they were conduits for meaningful content. Every element was meticulously chosen to align with the narrative. Our focus was on highlighting not just features, but the actual benefits that our solutions brought to the table.
The event's global nature required localization efforts. To accommodate a diverse audience, most sessions were made available in nine languages, making sure that language wasn't a barrier to participation.
