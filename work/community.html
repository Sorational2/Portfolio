Initial community was developed with a 3-rd party framework. It was a suitable solution at the beginning but with time it became challenging to customize, scale and maintain the extending content. With an increasing focus on user experience and a user-centred approach along with a need for alignment across development frameworks, the decision was made to redesign and migrate the Community platform.

The vision - to offer the best customer platform within digital dentistry offering the best user experience on the market​.

The Design Process

The whole project took 1.5 years, from the beginning to the launch. But the launch is not a success, so it's still ongoing. There are involved 7-10 people.
One of the challenges was information architecture - how to make all that immense amount of information discoverable and easy to use. During the wireframe stage, I realized that this project is about highly dense UI with a focus on fixing users' everyday problems and making it not a promo website with beautiful big hero images. We are not selling company products here. Probably.
